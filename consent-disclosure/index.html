Consent & TPMO Disclosure

The full contact consent you agreed to and the CMS Third-Party Marketing Organization disclosure.

Last updated: April 2026. When you check the consent box on a Medicare Moms contact form, this is the full agreement you are entering into.

1. TCPA Contact Consent

By checking the consent box on any Medicare Moms contact form and providing your phone number and email address, you authorize Medicare Moms, its licensed insurance agents, and its affiliates to contact you at the phone number(s) and email address(es) you provided.

How we may contact you

  • Phone calls, including those made using an automatic telephone dialing system (ATDS) or artificial or prerecorded voice
  • Text messages (SMS/MMS), including those sent using automated technology
  • Email messages

What we may contact you about

  • Medicare Advantage plans
  • Medicare Supplement (Medigap) plans
  • Prescription drug (Part D) plans
  • ACA / Marketplace plans
  • Dental, vision, life, accident, and critical illness insurance
  • Enrollment periods, plan reviews, and related marketing communications

Your rights

  • Consent is not a condition of purchase. You are not required to give this consent in order to purchase any insurance product from us.
  • Message and data rates may apply to text messages. Message frequency may vary.
  • Reply STOP to any text message to stop receiving SMS messages from us at any time. Reply HELP for help.
  • You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing info@medicaremoms.com, calling (435) 246-1548, or replying STOP to a text message.

2. TPMO Disclosure (Medicare Advantage & Part D)

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) requires all Third-Party Marketing Organizations (TPMOs) that sell Medicare Advantage and Part D plans to provide the following disclosure to every consumer:

We do not offer every plan available in your area. Any information we provide is limited to those plans we do offer in your area. Please contact Medicare.gov or 1-800-MEDICARE to get information on all of your options.

Medicare Moms is a licensed insurance agency that represents multiple insurance carriers. The specific carriers and plans we offer in your area can be provided upon request. You are always free to shop all plans available to you through Medicare.gov, your State Health Insurance Assistance Program (SHIP), or by calling 1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227), 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. TTY users should call 1-877-486-2048.

3. No Government Affiliation

Medicare Moms is not connected with, affiliated with, or endorsed by the U.S. government, the federal Medicare program, or any government agency. Medicare has neither reviewed nor endorsed the information on this website.

4. Recording Notice

For quality assurance, training, and compliance purposes, calls to and from Medicare Moms may be recorded. By continuing a call with one of our licensed agents, you consent to the recording of that call.

5. Privacy

Information you provide to Medicare Moms is handled according to our Privacy Policy. We do not sell your personal information. We share limited information only with licensed agents on our team, the insurance carriers whose plans you apply for, and service providers who help us operate (such as our CRM and communications platforms), all of whom are required to protect your information.

6. How to Opt Out

To stop all contact from Medicare Moms at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any text message from us
  • Click the unsubscribe link in any email from us
  • Email info@medicaremoms.com with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line
  • Call (435) 246-1548 during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm MT)

Opt-out requests are processed promptly. You may still receive messages directly related to an active application or policy you hold.
