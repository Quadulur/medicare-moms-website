4. Medicaid & CHIP Eligibility
  • Adults (expansion): up to 138% FPL41 states + DC as of 2026
  • Adults (non-expansion): well below 100% FPL; childless adults often ineligible
  • Pregnant women: 138–200% FPL typical; up to 300% in some states
  • Children (Medicaid): 138% FPL min; many states higher
  • CHIP (children): 170–400% FPL depending on state (median ~255%)
State eligibility varies. Source: Medicaid.gov, KFF.
5. Employer Coverage Affordability
9.96%

6. Catastrophic (Age 30+)
8.05%
Affordability exemption
Enrollees 30+ may purchase a catastrophic plan if lowest-cost Marketplace or employer coverage exceeds this % of household income.
Source: Federal Register 2025-11606.

8. Tax Filing Thresholds · TY 2025
  • Single: $15,750
  • Head of Household: $23,625
  • MFJ (both < 65): $31,500
  • Qualifying Surviving Spouse: $31,500
  • Married Filing Separately: any gross income
  • APTC note: Must file + Form 8962 regardless of thresholds.
