What You Can Offer

Here's the full menu. The key word is can. You're never captive with us, so sell what fits your life, skip what doesn't, and keep any books of business you already have somewhere else.

Medicare Advantage

All-in-one plans for seniors 65+. Bundles hospital, medical, Rx, and often dental/vision at $0 premium. This is your bread-and-butter product during Annual Enrollment (Oct 15 – Dec 7).

Medicare Supplements

Medigap plans that cover what Original Medicare doesn't. Great for clients who want to see any doctor nationwide. Sells year-round, no enrollment window needed.

Individual & Family Health

ACA Marketplace plans plus private and underwritten health insurance for individuals and families under 65. Open Enrollment runs Nov 1 – Dec 15, plus Special Enrollment throughout the year.

Dental, Vision & Supplemental

Coverage for teeth and eyes, plus accident and critical illness plans that pay cash directly to clients. Easy cross-sells that pair with any health plan.

Life Insurance & Long-Term Care

Term life, final expense, and long-term care coverage to protect families and plan for the future. Many plans don't require a medical exam.

Small & Large Group

Employer-sponsored health plans for local businesses. Recurring revenue and long-term client relationships.

We're not a captive agency. You're free to focus on just Medicare, just individual/ACA, or any mix that makes sense for you. Already sell life insurance somewhere else? Keep it there, no problem. You can do as much or as little business as fits your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need insurance experience?

▼

No, you don't need any experience at all. We provide full training covering Medicare, ACA, Medigap, and all our product lines. Many of our top agents started with zero insurance background. We teach you everything you need to know.

Can I do this part-time?

▼

Absolutely. Many of our agents start part-time and build at their own pace. This is a great way to add additional income to your household with a schedule that flexes around your family. You decide how much time to put in, there's no minimum hours requirement.

What does it cost to get started?

▼

We have programs in place to reimburse all of your licensing costs, so there's no real out-of-pocket barrier to getting started. We don't charge franchise fees, buy-in fees, or monthly software costs. We invest in you because we believe in you.

Can I really do this while raising kids?

▼

Absolutely. This is why Medicare Moms exists. Most of our agents are moms who work around their family's schedule. You control your hours. Work during school hours, early mornings, evenings, or weekends. Work part-time to start, and scale up as your kids get older. The flexibility is built into the model.

What support do I get?

▼

You get mentorship from experienced agents, comprehensive initial training plus ongoing education, professionally designed marketing materials, lead support to help you build your client base, and access to a community of women who understand the challenges of balancing motherhood and entrepreneurship. You're never alone in this.
